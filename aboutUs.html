About SHOGRO



The purpose is to encourage people to Spend their Money Where They Live and increase small business revenue and sustainability




ShoGro is online fundraising card that connects small businesses and community organizations. Non-profit community organizations become marketing ambassadors for local small businesses by selling Virtual Fundraising Cards to raise funds for organizations to provide community services.





These Virtual Fundraising Cards (VFC) would provide incentives for patrons to purchase deals for local businesses while serving the dual purpose of helping to raise funds for the organization.







ShoGro removes the overhead of physical fundraising cards for businesses, adds the ease of social media and online sharing, and increases mutual beneficial community partnerships between people, places, and things.
