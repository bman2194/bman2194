YouthInBiz (YIB) is a non-profit organization focused on the development of entrepreneurship and innovation skills in youth from underserved areas. With the youth unemployment rate rising into the double digits, decreasing high school graduation rates, and increasing truancy rates in schools, YouthInBiz needed to create a solution to address the need for youth to use time talent wisely. By concentrating on the development of youth and young adults ages 11-24 through creative, challenging, and innovative programming we can lessen the wealth and education gap that continues to devastate their futures.
